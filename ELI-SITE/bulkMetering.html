Bulk Metering Services

Eligugu helps you consolidate your property’s electricity meters into one efficient system, reducing costs and enhancing control. Save up to 40% on electricity bills through our smart bulk metering solutions.

What Eligugu Offers

  • Professional application and installation of your bulk meter
  • Complete electrical assessment before meter placement
  • Handle all municipal requirements and documentation
  • Set up advanced revenue management systems

Why Choose Bulk Metering?

  • Avoid estimated billing by Council/Eskom
  • Prevent overcharging for electricity
  • Eliminate tampering and illegal connections
  • Enjoy convenient and competitive energy management
