Simple, Transparent Installation Costs

Installing a prepaid meter involves only two costs:

Why Proper Installation Matters

  • Meters must be installed correctly to protect revenue.
  • Only meter readers are allowed on-site post-installation.
  • Installations follow national electrical standards.
  • Certificate of Compliance (CoC) is issued after installation.
